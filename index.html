About Us

The Awakening Angler YouTube Channel and Limp Lizard BBQ have joined forces to form WalleyeFest, LLC, bringing the annual first weekend in May Walleye Derby back to Oneida Lake. WalleyeFest Derby is not just an event; it's a tradition reborn that brings together families, anglers, and the entire community to celebrate the arrival of spring in Central New York.
